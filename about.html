How to use The "See The Sea Camera"

Introducing the "See The Sea camera" - a cutting-edge technology brought to you by Gandalf's AeroSpectra Emporium. To ensure a smooth experience, we've provided a step-by-step guide on how to effectively use your new camera. Follow the steps below to embark on a magical journey:

Step Step Number Description
Step 1 Go to the "Connect Us" page and purchase the camera.
Step 2 Once you receive the camera, use the serial number provided in the box to register a new account.
Step 3 Log in, navigate to the "Live View" page, and turn on the camera.
Step 4 The ESP32 will begin filming and transmit the video feed to our server.
Step 5 Our server will detect the number of individuals in the footage and then forward both the video and the count to the "Live View" page.
Final Step 6 You'll be able to view the number of people detected. Enjoy!
